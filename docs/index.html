Privacy Policy for X Viper Control

Last Updated: April 6, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how X Viper Control Skill ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our Alexa skill ("the Skill"). Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you use the Skill:

  • We require access to your vehicle remote service account credentials during the Amazon Alexa account linking process
  • These credentials are securely stored in Amazon Cognito, not directly in our Skill
  • We access information about vehicles registered to your account

Information Collected Automatically

When you interact with the Skill:

  • Voice commands processed by Amazon Alexa
  • Command timestamps
  • Command success/failure status
  • Device connectivity status

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Authenticate your identity with the remote vehicle service
  • Execute vehicle commands (lock, unlock, start engine, stop engine, open trunk)
  • Troubleshoot issues and improve Skill performance
  • Maintain appropriate security measures

We do not:

  • Store or log your voice recordings
  • Track your vehicle's location beyond what the remote service provides
  • Use your information for marketing purposes
  • Share your data with third parties beyond what's required for functionality

Data Storage and Security

  • Your account credentials are securely stored in Amazon Cognito
  • We implement AWS best practices for security
  • Data is encrypted during transmission and at rest
  • Access tokens are temporarily cached to improve performance
  • All cached data expires within a maximum of 2 hours

Your Rights

You can:

  • Disable the Skill at any time through the Alexa app
  • Unlink your account through the Alexa app

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted within the Skill description in the Alexa Skills Store.

Children's Privacy

The X Viper Control skill is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

AWS and Amazon Alexa Compliance

This Skill complies with the Amazon Alexa Skills terms of use and with AWS Service Terms. The Skill also follows Amazon's requirements regarding customer personal information.

By using the X Viper Control skill, you consent to our Privacy Policy and our processing of your information as set forth in this policy.
